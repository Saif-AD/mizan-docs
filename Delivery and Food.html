Delivery & food

Recommendations you can actually eat.

Every dish Mizan suggests is a real meal from our kitchen and partners, cooked, delivered, and logged for you.

How delivery works

From "you're low on iron" to a knock on the door.

1

Tap a recommendation

Every suggested dish shows what it closes, like "iron +44%", before you order. No menus to dig through.

2

We cook and deliver

Meals come from the Mizan kitchen and vetted partner restaurants, delivered by our own riders across the UAE.

3

Nutrients log themselves

When your order arrives, its macros and micros are logged automatically. Your rings move without you lifting a finger.

Sample meals

Why this meal? Every dish has an answer.

Grilled Beef & Spinach Bowl

Iron 8mg Protein 42g Vit C 60mg 610 kcal
Why this meal

Recommended when iron runs low. Beef brings heme iron, the kind your body absorbs best, and the roasted peppers add vitamin C, which can triple that absorption. One bowl, most of a day's iron.

Charred Salmon & Freekeh

Vit D 12µg Omega-3 1.8g Protein 38g 560 kcal
Why this meal

Built for the most common gap we see in the UAE: vitamin D. Oily fish is one of the only foods that meaningfully moves it, and the omega-3s come along for free. Freekeh adds slow-release carbs and fibre.

Lentil, Date & Halloumi Salad

Folate 180µg Calcium 320mg Fibre 11g 490 kcal
Why this meal

For plant-forward days and B-vitamin gaps. Lentils carry folate and plant iron, halloumi covers calcium, and dates bring the sweetness without the crash. The lemon dressing isn't decoration. It's there for absorption.
