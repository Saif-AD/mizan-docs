How it works & the science

No guesswork. Just your numbers, working for you.

How Mizan turns a blood test into daily targets, recommendations, and a meal at your door.

The loop

Four steps, one continuous cycle.

1

Connect your bloodwork

Link your verified lab results through our secured UAE government integration, or upload results from your clinic. Mizan reads the markers that matter for nutrition, like iron studies, vitamin D, B12, folate and lipids, directly from the source, so there's no typing and no transcription errors. A test from the last 12 months is enough to start.

2

Get personalized targets

Generic guidelines assume an average body. Yours isn't average. Mizan converts each marker into a daily intake target: low ferritin raises your iron goal, low 25(OH)D raises vitamin D, your training load and body composition shape protein. The result is a daily plan of macros and micros that belongs to exactly one person: you.

3

See live recommendations

As you log meals, Mizan tracks what's still missing today and scores real dishes against your shortfall. It doesn't just say "eat more iron". It shows the beef and spinach bowl that closes the gap, and pairs it with vitamin C so your body actually absorbs it. Recommendations update with every meal you eat.

4

Order delivery

Every recommendation is a real dish from our kitchen and partner restaurants, delivered by Mizan across the UAE. Tap to order, and the nutrients are logged automatically when it arrives. The loop closes: your next blood test shows the difference, and your targets adjust again.

From bloodwork to targets

How a marker becomes a meal.

A few examples of how your results translate into daily goals.

Ferritin & haemoglobin → Low ferritin raises your daily iron target and prioritizes heme-iron dishes, paired with vitamin C for absorption.
25(OH) vitamin D → Below-range vitamin D raises your daily D target and weights oily fish, eggs and fortified foods in your recommendations.
B12 & folate → Borderline B12, common with plant-forward diets, lifts your B12 goal and surfaces the foods that cover it.
Lipid profile → Your cholesterol pattern shapes the balance of fats in your plan: more omega-3s, fewer saturated-fat-heavy dishes.
HbA1c & fasting glucose → Trending glucose markers shift your carbohydrate targets toward higher-fibre, slower-release sources.

Mizan supports your nutrition decisions. It doesn't diagnose or treat. Out-of-range results always come with a prompt to speak to your doctor.

The UAE government integration

Verified at the source. Limited by design.

Mizan connects to the UAE's secured health-data platform so your results arrive verified, exactly as the lab reported them.

placeholder: integration specifics being finalized

What it does

  • Retrieves your lab results, with your explicit consent, each time you ask it to.
  • Verifies results came from an accredited UAE laboratory.
  • Confirms your identity through the official UAE Pass flow, so you log in with them, not with us.

What it never accesses

  • Your medical history, diagnoses, prescriptions or visit notes.
  • Your Emirates ID credentials. We never see your login.
  • Anything without a fresh, revocable consent from you.

Privacy in depth

Stored like it matters. Because it does.

🔒

Encrypted, twice

Results are encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256), the same standards used in banking. Bloodwork values are stored separately from your identity.

⏻

You hold the switch

Consent is granular and revocable. Disconnect the government integration, delete a test, or erase your account and all health data. Each takes one tap, and deletion is permanent.

✕

Never sold, never shared

Your data is never sold to advertisers or shared with insurers or employers. Aggregated, anonymized statistics only ever leave with your separate opt-in.
